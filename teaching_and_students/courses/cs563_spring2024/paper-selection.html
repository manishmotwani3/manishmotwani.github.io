Below, you will find a list of papers. Each paper has a specific presentation day associated with it. You must sign up to present one paper.

The paper presentations will be done either individually or in groups of up to two students. The groups will be determined by who signs up for each paper. (Thus, if it is very important to you to work with specific friend, coordinate signing up for the same paper with the friend.)

To sign up, add yourself to one of the "Paper Selection" groups at https://canvas.oregonstate.edu/courses/1964338/groups#tab-110068. The group numbers correspond to the paper id in the below table. 

Sign ups will open Tuesday, April 9, 2024, 11:00 AM PT and will be done on a first-come first-served basis.

There are 10 papers to be presented this term. Each paper has a presentation date associated with it. All paper presentations will be 20 minutes followed by 5 minutes of Q/A and discussion. Every paper will end up with a team of up to two students to work as a team for the presentation. All presentations will be during regular class times. You will be given more guidance on how to create slides and present a research paper.

Choosing the papers can take a bit of time. To make the most well-educated choices, you should take at least a brief look at each paper and read its abstract, introduction, and conclusion sections. Each paper below includes a URL. Some papers are available for free, and others are available for free only to you as OSU students.  The links will just work if you are on campus, or connected via the OSU VPN if you are off-campus.
